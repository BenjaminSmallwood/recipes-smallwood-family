---
title: "Classic Caesar Salad"
category: "Salads"
image: "/images/caesar-salad.jpg"
cookTime: "0 min"
prepTime: "20 min"
servings: 4
tags: ["salad", "lunch", "easy"]
ingredients:
  - name: "romaine lettuce"
    quantity: 2
    unit: "heads"
  - name: "parmesan cheese"
    quantity: 0.5
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "croutons"
    quantity: 1
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "olive oil"
    quantity: 0.33
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "lemon juice"
    quantity: 3
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "garlic"
    quantity: 2
    unit: "cloves"
  - name: "dijon mustard"
    quantity: 1
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "anchovy paste"
    quantity: 1
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "egg yolk"
    quantity: 1
    unit: ""
  - name: "black pepper"
    quantity: 0.5
    unit: "tsp"
---

1. Wash and chop {romaine lettuce} into bite-sized pieces.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together {olive oil}, {lemon juice}, minced {garlic}, {dijon mustard}, {anchovy paste}, and {egg yolk}.
3. Season the dressing with {black pepper}.
4. Toss the lettuce with the dressing until evenly coated.
5. Top with freshly grated {parmesan cheese} and {croutons}.
6. Serve immediately.
