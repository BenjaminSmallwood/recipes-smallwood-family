---
title: "Grandma's Chicken Pot Pie"
category: "Main Dishes"
image: "/images/chicken-pot-pie.jpg"
cookTime: "45 min"
prepTime: "30 min"
servings: 6
tags: ["chicken", "comfort food", "dinner"]
ingredients:
  - name: "chicken breast"
    quantity: 2
    unit: "lbs"
  - name: "butter"
    quantity: 4
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "all-purpose flour"
    quantity: 0.33
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "chicken broth"
    quantity: 1.75
    unit: "cups"
  - name: "milk"
    quantity: 0.67
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "frozen mixed vegetables"
    quantity: 2
    unit: "cups"
  - name: "salt"
    quantity: 1
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "black pepper"
    quantity: 0.5
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "pie crust"
    quantity: 2
    unit: ""
---

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Cook and shred {chicken breast}.
3. In a large saucepan, melt {butter} over medium heat.
4. Stir in {all-purpose flour} and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
5. Gradually whisk in {chicken broth} and {milk}. Cook until thickened, about 5 minutes.
6. Season with {salt} and {black pepper}.
7. Stir in {frozen mixed vegetables} and the shredded chicken.
8. Line a 9-inch pie dish with one {pie crust}.
9. Pour the filling into the crust and top with the second {pie crust}. Crimp edges and cut slits for steam.
10. Bake for 30-35 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
11. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
